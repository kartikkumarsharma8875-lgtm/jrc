Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for admission?

You need to provide: Photo ID proof (Aadhar/Passport), 2 passport size photos, and parent's contact details. Local guardian details may be required for outstation students.

Are meals included in the rent?

Yes, all our packages include 3 times meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner). We serve healthy, home-cooked vegetarian food. Special dietary requirements can be discussed.

What are the rules and regulations?

We maintain a peaceful environment with reasonable rules: No smoking/drinking, guests allowed with prior permission, maintain cleanliness, respect other residents, and follow entry/exit timings.

Is there a notice period for leaving?

Yes, we require a 20-day notice period before leaving. Security deposit will be refunded after room inspection and any pending dues clearance.
